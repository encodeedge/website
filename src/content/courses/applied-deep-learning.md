AI seems like magic, but it’s really just math and code—and you don't need a PhD to figure it out. This course takes the intimidation factor out of deep learning.

We’ll start with the absolute basics and work our way up to building real neural networks using modern framework. We keep the academic jargon to a minimum and focus on how things actually work. By the end, you won't just understand the hype; you’ll have coded your own models.
